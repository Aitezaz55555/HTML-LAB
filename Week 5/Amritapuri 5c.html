About Amritapuri Campus

The Amritapuri campus, located in Kerala near the scenic backwaters and Arabian Sea, is the spiritual headquarters of Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham. It offers programs in Engineering, Arts & Sciences, Ayurveda, and Research, blending modern education with traditional values and holistic development.

Facilities

  • Advanced laboratories and research centers
  • Library with digital and print collections
  • Hostels and student housing
  • Sports and cultural activity spaces
  • Medical and wellness facilities
