About Bangalore Campus

The Bangalore campus of Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham is located in Karnataka’s tech hub. It is known for its strong emphasis on Engineering, Management, Computer Science, and interdisciplinary research. The campus fosters innovation and industry collaboration, making it a preferred destination for students aspiring to excel in technology and business.

Facilities

  • State-of-the-art computer labs and research centers
  • Library with digital and physical resources
  • Hostels and student housing
  • Sports and fitness facilities
  • Medical and wellness services
