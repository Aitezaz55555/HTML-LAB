About Amaravati Campus

The Amaravati campus of Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham is located in Andhra Pradesh, in the upcoming capital city region. It is envisioned as a hub for cutting-edge education and research, offering programs in Engineering, Management, Arts & Sciences, and emerging interdisciplinary fields.

Facilities

  • Smart classrooms and advanced laboratories
  • Digital library and e-learning resources
  • Student hostels and residential facilities
  • Sports grounds and fitness centers
  • Healthcare and wellness services
