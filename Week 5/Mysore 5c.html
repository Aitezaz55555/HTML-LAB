About Mysore Campus

The Mysore campus of Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham is located in Karnataka, offering programs in Computer Science, Management, Arts & Sciences, and emerging fields. The campus emphasizes academic excellence, research, and holistic student development in a serene learning environment.

Facilities

  • Modern classrooms and computer labs
  • Library with digital and print resources
  • Student hostels and residential facilities
  • Sports and recreational amenities
  • Healthcare and wellness services
