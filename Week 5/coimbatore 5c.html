About Coimbatore Campus

The Coimbatore campus is the headquarters of Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham. It is located at Ettimadai, in a scenic area surrounded by the Western Ghats. The campus offers a wide range of programs in Engineering, Business, Arts & Sciences, and Research.

Facilities

  • Modern classrooms and laboratories
  • Central library with extensive resources
  • Hostels and student housing
  • Sports and recreational facilities
  • Medical center and wellness services
