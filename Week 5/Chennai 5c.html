About Chennai Campus

The Chennai campus of Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham is located in Tamil Nadu’s capital city. It focuses on Engineering, Computer Science, Business, and interdisciplinary programs, fostering innovation and industry partnerships. The campus provides a vibrant environment for students to excel academically and professionally.

Facilities

  • Modern classrooms and advanced laboratories
  • Library with digital and physical resources
  • Student hostels and residential facilities
  • Sports and recreational amenities
  • Healthcare and wellness services
